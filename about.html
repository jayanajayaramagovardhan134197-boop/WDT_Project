✦ Who We Are

We Are Trip Zone

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate explorers, Trip Zone was born out of a simple belief: every person deserves a perfectly crafted travel experience. What began as a boutique travel agency in Mumbai has grown into one of India's most trusted premium travel platforms.

We curate handpicked itineraries across India and 100+ countries, blending cultural authenticity with modern comfort. Our expert travel consultants — each a seasoned traveller themselves — ensure that every journey we craft tells a story worth remembering.

✦ Our Journey

How We Grew

From a small team of three with big dreams to a 200-person company serving travellers across India and beyond.

2018
Trip Zone Founded

Three travel enthusiasts launched Trip Zone from a Mumbai co-working space with a simple website and a big dream.

2019
10,000 Customers Milestone

Crossed our first major milestone and opened offices in Delhi and Bangalore. Launched signature Rajasthan Royal packages.

2021
International Expansion

Expanded to international destinations across Southeast Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. Launched visa assistance service.

2023
Trip Zone App Launch

Launched our mobile app with AI-powered trip planning. Reached 30,000 active users in the first month.

2025
50,000 Happy Travellers

Ranked #1 luxury travel platform in India by Travel+Leisure India. 50,000 journeys completed.
