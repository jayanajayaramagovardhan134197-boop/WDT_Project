✦ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer? Reach us directly using the contact form above.

How long does it take to receive a response?
We typically respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent travel needs, please call our 24/7 toll-free number: 1800-000-WANDER.
Can I customise a travel package?
Absolutely! All our packages can be fully customised to suit your budget, travel dates, group size, dietary requirements, and personal interests. Use the contact form and select "Custom Itinerary" as the subject.
Do you offer visa assistance?
Yes, WanderLux provides end-to-end visa assistance for all international destinations. Our team handles documentation, appointment scheduling, and application submission for most countries.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 30+ days before travel receive a full refund. 15–29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund unless covered by travel insurance. We always recommend purchasing travel insurance.
Is there a travel insurance option?
Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance through our partner insurers covering trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost baggage, and flight delays. Ask our team for details when booking.
How do I book a cab or airport transfer?
Visit our Flights & Cabs page to book airport transfers and city cabs. You can also mention it in the contact form and our team will arrange it as part of your package.
